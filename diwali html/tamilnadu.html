Diwali in Tamilnadu

The festival of lights in Tamilnadu is called Deepavali. For a Tamilan, a Deepavali celebration starts with an early morning oil bath and ends with bursting crackers at night. Textile shops and sweet shops will be flooded by Diwali shopping customers. It is the peak business season for the firecracker industries in Sivakasi. The festival falls in the month of October/November.
